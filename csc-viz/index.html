Emojicloud

This emojicloud represents all emojis that Collyn Chan and I used in our WhatsApp conversations since September 2018. Using the TextBlob natural language processing package in python, I extracted and counted emojis from our conversation.

Emojicloud: school

By matching times from my Google Maps Timeline history to message times, I extracted emojis from messages sent while I was at MIT from 2/11/19 - 2/13/19.

Emojicloud: home

Using the same technique, I extracted emojis sent when I was home over the same 3 days.
